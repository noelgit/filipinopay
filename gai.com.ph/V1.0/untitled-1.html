Great Automation Incorporated

by Bayani asuncion jr.march 3, 2019


Once known as Great Automation Sales and Services, which offers
high-quality products to our customers, GREAT AUTOMATION INCORPORATED was born
out of the increase in demands of customers who not only looking for market
products but who wanted customized products and services that suits their
evolving business requirements. To Further advance our foothold in the
industry, we have a sister company, iTECH Industrial Sales & Fabrication
whose expertise range from various Fabrication of customized Panel Boards.

From a small group of 10 employees, we have grown to close to 100 professional
personnel that can attend to any Electrical, Engineering, and IT related
requirements with a common passion of total customer satisfaction and aligning
it to our goal of “Changing Filipino lives, One Project at a Time”.

January of 2019 added another milestone to Great Automation Incorporated when we signed the  JOINT VENTURE Agreement between Development Bank of the Philippines Data Center
Incorporated (DBP-DCI),  a Government Owned and Controlled Corporation
under the office of the President, to provide a wide range of solution fit for
the Philippine government needs such as but not limited to System Integration,
Building Automation, Smart Structure, Data Center Management, Service
Maintenance, Online Payment Gateway, Software Development, Smart City Safe City
with Facial and Vehicle recognition system, RFID, medium voltage switch gears,
Low voltage switchgear, Transformer, PLC, VFD, pumps, Generators, wires and
cables and other related electro-mechanical products.
